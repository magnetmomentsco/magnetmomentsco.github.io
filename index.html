Our Story

Hi, I'm Alyssa

& I'm the heart behind Magnet Moments Co.

This business began with a simple desire to bring my favorite family photos out of my phone and into my home in a way that felt intentional and meaningful. What started as a personal project quickly became a passion for helping others display the moments that matter most.

"My goal is to craft keepsakes that help you pause, reflect, and relive the emotions captured in your photos."

My husband and two sons are the driving force behind everything I do. Seeing the joy on my boys' faces when they spot themselves on a magnet reminded me how powerful it is to reconnect with our memories in a tangible way.
